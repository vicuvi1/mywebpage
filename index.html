// about

      
Who I am

    

    

      

        
I'm Victor Barbuta, a cybersecurity practitioner driven by a simple belief: to defend a system, you have to think like the person attacking it.

        
My focus is offensive security and threat analysis, but I care just as much about the defensive side — because finding a flaw only matters if you can help fix it. I spend my time in labs, CTFs, and hands-on projects, sharpening skills across the whole security stack.

        
I'm early in an ambitious journey and I move fast: every box owned, every writeup published, and every certification earned is a step toward the specialist I'm building toward.
